2020 TOKYO OLYMPICS AT A GLANCE

Total Medals by Country

As seen in the map below, countries are highlighted in different shade of red to project the number of medals the country has won in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics. The scale used to show the number of medals has the darkest shade representing countries that has won the most medals.

The United States of America is coloured with the darkest shade of red indicating that the country has won the most medals in the Olympics game. On the other hand, countries like Morocco, Saudi Arabia and more has the lightest shade of red representing that the countries have won 1 medal.Countries that are shaded with grey indicates that either the country did not participate in 2020 Tokyo Olympics or the countries did not win any medals.

Top 8 Countries in the 5 Recent Olympics

United States has always been in the first place, winning over 100 pieces of medals for 5 Olympics consecutively.

United Kingdom was not in the top 8 in 2004's Olympics but it has made it to the top 8 from 2008's Olympics onwards. Since then, the country has kept its place in the top 4.

South Korea has only made it to the top 8 once in the past 5 Olympics, which was the 8th place in 2008's Olympics.

Also worth mentioning, Italy only made it to the top 8 in 2004 and 2020's Olympics. The country made its comeback to the top 8 in 2020 Tokyo Olympics by ranking 7th on the list.


Medal Breakdown for the Top 8 Countries in 2020 Tokyo Olympics

The United States won more Silver medals than Gold and Bronze medals in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics. Most of the Silver medals are from the Swimming and Athletics events.

On the other hand, China has won more Gold medals than Silver and Bronze medals. The events that contributed the most to the count are Diving and Weightlifting.

Australia's Performance According to Sport Types

Australia has always performed well in the Water Sports category especially Swimming. Other than that, they also thrive in Canoe, Sailing and Rowing.

In 2016, the Aussies has done exceptionally well in Archery and Modern Pentathlon, which is categories under Multi-Sports. Chloe Esposito, the woman in the first picture on the right, has won Gold medal in the Women Modern Pentathlon event.

Australia has gotten a Bronze medal in the Men Boxing event in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics alongside with a Silver and a Bronze medal in Equestrian events.

China's Performance According to Sport Types

China has alwyas been known for their skills in Badminton and Table Tennis, which is why they are thriving in the Racket Sports catergory in 2016 and 2020's Olympics. Some of their famous Badminton players include Lin Dan and Chen Long. As for Table Tennis, Ma Long is very well known as well.

Besides Racket Sports, China also does well in the Gymnastics and Shooting Sports categories. China has won 11 medals for the Shooting aspect in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics.

China also performed exceptionally well in the Weightlifting and Water Sports area, especially Diving. Just looking at the statistics for Diving, China has won 7 Golds and 5 Silvers.

France's Performance According to Sport Types

In 2016, France has performed well in Equestrian as they have won 2 Golds in the Team Open events. As for Individual event, Astier Nicolas has won a Silver medal for the Open Equestrian Eventing.

As for 2020 Tokyo Olympics, France has thrived in Judo, categories under Combat Sports, as the Women's team contributed 5 medals to the team and the Men won 2 medals. Amongst the five medals from the Women's division, there was one Gold won by Clarisse Agbegnenou.

It is also worth highlighting that both the Men and Women Basketball team has won medals for the country.

United States's Performance According to Sport Types

The Americans are very consistent in their performance when it comes to the Athletics events as they have won 32 and 26 medals in the 2016 and 2020 Olympics respectively.

The Men and Women Basketball team have both won Gold in the Basketball events for two consecutive Olympics games.

In 2020 Tokyp Olympics, the Americans have won first place in the Men and Women Individual Golf events.

The 2016 Olympics gold medalist for Men's Heavyweight Freestyle Wrestling, Kyle Synder, has competed in the same event in 2020 Tokyo Olympics and won a Silver medal for the country.
