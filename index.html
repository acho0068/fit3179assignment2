2020 Tokyo Olympics at a Glance

Total Medals by Countries

As seen in the map above, countries are highlighted in different colour to project the number of medals the country has won in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics games. The scale used to show the number of medals are broke down into 6 shades, with the darkest representing countries that has won more than 100 medals.
